## Top Features Worth Stealing

1. **CSR AI** - AI answers phones and books jobs 24/7
2. **Instapay** - 30-minute payment deposits
3. **Customer Portal** - Self-service for history, payments, booking
4. **Good-Better-Best Estimates** - Side-by-side proposal layouts
5. **Property Profiles** - Equipment tracking per site
6. **Analyst AI** - Natural language report generation
7. **Offline Mode** - Full functionality without internet
8. **Auto-charge on Completion** - Seamless payment collection
